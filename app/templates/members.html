Conner Swann

Electric Triangle and Gnarly Vocals

Conner has been with Beathoven for a little under a week now, he has great ambitions in the world of music and hopes to one day perform at each and every TGI Friday's franchise in Northern Arizona.

His interests include Computer Programming, Hip Hop dancing, and hustling children out of their lunch money.

Justin Poehnelt

Bass Guitar

Justin started playing music yesterday. The bass guitar works well for him since he can pretend to play and no one can tell the difference. He's hoping he never has a solo...

Justin loves the outdoors. He and his wife are often on an adventure exploring northern Arizona.

Mitchell Hewitt

Drums

Mitchell is an expert at the boring and repetitive. Naturally, this makes playing the same three notes on the drums for every song a breeze. With enough luck, he will never have to learn another note.

Mitchell likes to spend his free time wherever he can find peace and silence.

Herbie Duah

Beat Sampler

Herbie is an experimentalist. He managed to live his whole life not hearing a single note of classical music until one he heard some Beethoven. After he heard it, he knew the world of hip hop and classical needed to join.

He spents his time partying it up! Or down or sideways.
